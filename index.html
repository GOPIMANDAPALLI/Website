About Our Project

Our CSP project focuses on studying Internet usage in Gurralavari Palli village. We surveyed villagers to understand their level of digital literacy, how they access the Internet, and how it impacts their education, communication, and livelihood.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Empower rural communities with digital literacy for a smarter and connected India.
Mission: Promote awareness of Internet usage for learning, agriculture, business, and social development.

Awareness Session

We organized an awareness session in Gurralavari Palli to educate villagers about safe, smart, and productive Internet usage. The session included live demonstrations, printed materials, and video tutorials in Telugu and English.
